About Me

I am a London based Software Developer.

I began coding as a hobby during the initial stages of the lockdown, I used websites such as freeCodeCamp and MDN Web Docs which helped me solidify my understanding of HTML and CSS. I am currently studying on the Tech Talent Accelerator programme with Generation UK to help prepare me for an apprenticeship or a junior role as a Software Developer.

I previously worked as a Finance Assistant and then as an External Auditor, I realised that the path I was upon was not really suited for me. I believe that a career in Software Development will allow me to make a positive impact on people which is one of my main objectives in life.
